Our Achievements

Club Achievements
Player Achievements

Winner of Smriti Cup 2024

Our club emerged victorious in the Thakur Harivansh Singh Smriti Cup , showcasing unparalleled teamwork and sportsmanship. Unlike previous year this time our team snatched this cup by beating 11 star in finals by 1-0. This win marks a significant milestone in the club's journey and has set a benchmark for future campaigns.

Smriti Cup 2023 - Runner Up

In 2023 , our club successfully hosted the widely recognized Thakur Harivansh Singh Smriti Cup featuring over 16 competitive teams from whole district and other region . It was an event filled with enthusiasm, passion, and competitive spirit . Our own team participated in it and reached final of this tournament emerged as runner up . The tournament gained attention for its professional organization, vibrant crowd, and thrilling matches, boosting our reputation as both a formidable team and an excellent host.

District Tournament 2018 - Shared Winners Trophy

Our Sub-Juniour team emerged as and outstanding and youngest players team to touch the winners trophy. It was an tournament filled with enthusiasm, passion, and competitive spirit . The final was very competitive and was against Police Boys Bandol where we Drew the match with 0-0 even after the extra time . This trophy gave huge motivation to the future champions and 7 players of our team were selcted for the State championship held at Khandwa .

Anosh Baba Das

Baba has been one of our most consistent performers over the years. He won Gold medal for Nationals and had played 4 times Nationals and 7 times state. He has trained many of the players during camps.

Priyanshu Singh Chouhan

Priyanshu has been one of our best and most valuable player and one of the gratest defender of our club. He got gold medal for Nationals and had played 5 times nationals and over 8 times state. He has also represented Jivaji university on various National and State competitions. Priyanshu is also the Licensed Coach and AIFF 'D' License holder.

Aman Vishwakarma

Aman is the most finest and best Goalkeeper of our club. He played 2 nationals and 5 states till now. He has won many university and district championships.

Justin Das

Justin’s dedication and fitness levels earned him a spot in the National Football Camp 2021. He has played over 2 times Nationals and 7 states and performed outstanding . He has also played for MPFA and won various medals.

Divyam Pandey

Divyam has been one of our finest mid player . He has played over 2 times Nationals and 5 times state. He also represented his college on various tournaments including IIT BHU and has won many medals.
